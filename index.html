Who are you?

The Utah Ruby Users Group (URUG) is a mostly informal collection of individuals interested in the Ruby programming language.

When do you meet?

We have several chapters, or brigades.

How do I join?

URUG membership incurs no dues, no fees, and no obligation. Just show up to the meetings periodically, and you're welcome to call yourself a member of the URUG.

Most of our activities are on the Meetup page.
